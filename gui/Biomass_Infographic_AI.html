Why DINOv2?

Traditional CNNs struggle with the "texture" of biomass (dense vs. sparse grass). DINOv2 is a self-supervised Vision Transformer that understands semantic object properties without needing massive labeled datasets.

Why EfficientNet?

It captures local high-frequency details (edges of leaves) that Transformers might miss. The ensemble provides the best of both worlds.

Modeling the "Zero"

Tweedie Loss (p=1.5)

Standard MSE loss fails when 30% of your data is exactly zero. We use Tweedie Loss, a compound Poisson-Gamma distribution.
